Welcome!

With FormBuilder, you can design your own forms by following three simple steps:

  1. Click the New Form button.
  2. Drag & drop form components from the right site of the screen into the left side.
  3. Press the Save button whenever you want to save your progress.

You can come back to this page by clicking the My Forms button in the top bar. It will then show you a list of all your forms.
